Dear Listener,

What you are about to hear is an exhibition created for Archipelago, airing from the 24th of June 2016. As a soundtrack for the summer, you can download the exhibition to your phone or laptop and listen to the mixtape where ever you may find yourself this holiday.

The tracks on this mixtape are newly commissioned works by seven artists and writers; Kristin Austreid, Ingrid Furre, Jason-Hendrik Hansma, Audun Mortensen, Rosalie Schweiker, Femke de Vries, and Inger Wold Lund. The various nature of their works and interests brings us a sequential of pieces from research extracts, poetry, field recording, narratives and carefully constructed soundtracks.

Archipelago is a small, flexible exhibition platform at Hordaland Kunstsenter (link) in Bergen. The mixtape is curated by Mathijs van Geest, and is supported by Bergen Kommune and Norsk Kulturr√•d.
